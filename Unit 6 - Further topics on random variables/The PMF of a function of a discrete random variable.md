
## Derived distributions - discrete case
$$
Y = g(x)
$$
![[Pasted image 20241019151845.png]]
$$
P_Y(4) = P_Y(Y=4)
$$
$$
= P(X = 5) + P(X = 4) = 0.4 + 0.3 = 0.7
$$
$$
p_Y(y)=P(g(X) = y) = \sum_{x:g(x)=y}p_X(x)
$$
